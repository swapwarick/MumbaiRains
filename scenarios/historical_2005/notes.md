# Historical 2005 Mumbai Deluge Scenario
Re-enactment of the extreme rainstorm event on 26 July 2005.
This scenario loads observed rainfall time series directly from `rainfall.csv`.
Includes a mean sea level offset of 0.5m, spring tide of 4.8m, storm surge of 0.8m,
and a 20% drainage network clogging factor due to urban debris accumulation.
